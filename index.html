Controls

Use the following keys to control your vehicle:

Up Arrow or W - Accelerate

Down Arrow or S - Brake

Left Arrow or A - Turn Left

Right Arrow or D - Turn Right

Space - Toggle Headlights

H - Sound Car Horn

Airplane Controls

When using the Airplane, you get additional flying controls:

Q - Fly Up

E - Fly Down

R - Pitch Up (nose up)

F - Pitch Down (nose down)

Z - Roll Left (bank left)

C - Roll Right (bank right)
