Controls

Use the following keys to control your vehicle:

Up Arrow or W - Accelerate

Down Arrow or S - Brake

Left Arrow or A - Turn Left

Right Arrow or D - Turn Right

Space - Toggle Headlights

H - Sound Car Horn

Game Features

• Collect coins around the track to earn points

• Different coins have different values

• Heart items increase your shield protection

• Race against other players

• Try different vehicles with unique capabilities
